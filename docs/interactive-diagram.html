System Components

TRAIN (5.6B Online)

Digital natives generating clickstream, geolocation, and telemetry data. Trained via Centaur AI and Stanford personality models.

ACQUIRE (2.8B Offline)

Limited-connectivity populations monitored via orbital imaging (10cm resolution) and ground-based facial recognition networks.

Fusion layer combining online and offline signals through exascale compute fabric. Produces real-time forecasts with confidence bands.

FEEDBACK Loop

Weaponization surface enabling policy micro-targeting, kinetic ops, and economic manipulation. Reinforcement data flows back to training.
